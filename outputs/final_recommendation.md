# Final Recommendation

- **Strongest factors:** Footfall and Marketing Spend drive sales the most.  
- **Leadership focus:** Increase customer traffic and invest wisely in marketing.  
- **Caution:** Do not over-interpret discount %, competitor distance, or staff count.  
- **Business action:** Focus on boosting store visits, optimize marketing, and avoid heavy discounting.  
- **Risks:** Model is linear and may miss local effects.  
- **Note:** Regression shows association, not causation — higher spend or footfall is linked to sales, but other hidden factors may also play a role.
